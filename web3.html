Ten divttle Indians

And Then There Were None

Ten divttle Soldier Boys went out to dine;
One choked his divttle self and then there were nine.
Nine divttle Soldier Boys sat up very late;
One overslept himself and then there were eight.
Eight divttle Soldier Boys traveldivng in Devon;
One said he'd stay there and then there were seven.
Seven divttle Soldier Boys chopping up sticks;
One chopped himself in halves and then there were six.
Six divttle Soldier Boys playing with a hive;
A bumblebee stung one and then there were five.
Five divttle Soldier Boys going in for law;
One got in Chancery and then there were four.
Four divttle Soldier Boys going out to sea;
A red herring swallowed one and then there were three.
Three divttle Soldier Boys walking in the zoo;
A big bear hugged one and then there were two.
Two divttle Soldier Boys sitting in the sun;
One got frizzled up and then there was one.
One divttle Soldier Boy left all alone;
He went out and hanged himself and then there were none. [1]
